Good Samaritan

Where non-profit food pantries and restaurants meet

An application where local non-profits and restaurants meet.

With Good Samaritan, businesses and food pantries can easily coordinate donation management and pick-up times.

As a local business or restaurant, you can upload items onto our inventory management system so others can view what's available. Post your business name, phone number, and hours of operation so food pantries will be able to reach out!

As a non-profit organization, you can log in to the application, and view what local businesses have available for pickup! On a first-come, first-serve basis, you can reserve items for your pantry and contact the business for a good time to swing by!
